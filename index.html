  • Clubs

    Clubs from the school have started. The clubs are very cool! Here are a few.




    Drawing Club


    Do you like to draw? Do you want to learn how to improve your drawing skills? Then this is the club for you! We will work on improving your skills at drawing from observation to create realistic artworks. Students will learn how to create value using several different techniques including crosshatching and stipple. So if you want to become a better draftsman, then come join the Drawing Club!




    Turn the Page - A Book Club
    Students of all ability levels will have the opportunity to read a variety of pre-selected novels. There will be a focus on the story elements, as well as several critical thinking skills. All pre-selected novels will also be available on Learning Ally, as to include special education students. Members will be able to practice important oral expressive language skills and take risks, while contributing to a group discussion with confidence.
